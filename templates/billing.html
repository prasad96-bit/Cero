Why Manual Billing?

This system uses manual billing confirmation to ensure longevity. Unlike automated systems that depend on third-party payment processors, manual billing allows the system to continue operating even if external services change or disappear. Your subscription is managed locally and securely, with grace periods to ensure uninterrupted service.
